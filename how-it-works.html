How Helpora Works

Helpora makes it easier to discover causes and donate directly to NGOs.

1

Discover

Browse NGOs by cause, region, or mission.

2

Review

Check NGO profile, registration details, and official links.

3

Donate

Use the NGO’s official donation channel. Helpora does not collect money.

4

Follow Impact

Read updates and stories shared by NGOs.

Important: Donors should independently verify tax benefit, FCRA eligibility, registration status, and official donation details before giving.
