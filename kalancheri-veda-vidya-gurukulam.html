Three Pillars of Kalancheri

Veda Adhyayanam

Dedicated to the oral tradition of Krishna Yajur and Sama Veda, preserving ancient wisdom through rigorous study and chanting.

Gho Samrakshanam

Sacred cow protection and care, including shelter, nourishment, and medical support for native breeds.

Rishi Krishi

Vedic agriculture using organic, cow-based, mantra-infused practices that nurture the land and support the gurukulam ecosystem.

Support Needs

The organization highlights these support areas on its official website.

Daily Samaradhanai

Support nutritious sattvic meals for 100+ students dedicated to Vedic learning.

Goshala Maintenance

Support fodder, housing, medicinal herbs, and veterinary care for sacred cows.

Vidyarthi Adoption

Sponsor a student’s education, boarding, books, traditional clothing, and learning materials.
