Vision & Mission

A global home for good causes, built on direct giving and transparency.

Vision

To become a global home for good causes, where donors can discover credible NGOs and support meaningful work directly, transparently, and confidently.

Mission

Helpora’s mission is to connect donors with credible NGOs across the world by organizing good causes in one trusted platform and enabling direct donations to the organizations doing the work.

Transparency

Clear NGO information and donation details.

Direct Giving

Donors give directly to the chosen NGO.

Compassion

A simple way to support causes globally.

Accountability

Encouraging updates, documents, and impact stories.
